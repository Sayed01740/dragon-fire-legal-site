🛡️ Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII). All game progress and settings are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

2. Wallet & Blockchain Transparency

We use your public wallet address solely to verify **Solana Gaming Pass** ownership. Transactions are recorded on the public Solana blockchain and are visible to anyone via ledger explorers.

3. Data Storage

Level progress, XP, and inventory are stored locally on your device. Clearing your cache will reset this data unless synced via a future cloud update. Pass ownership, however, is permanent on-chain.

4. Third-Party Services

We use **Helius RPC** for blockchain communication. No personal data is shared with Helius beyond the public address required for verification. We do not use tracking pixels or cookies.
